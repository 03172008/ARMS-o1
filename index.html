About Us

ARMS (Automobile Rescue Mobile Services) was founded by the Kosgi Family and Raja Chandra Gajula to provide reliable and trustworthy roadside assistance in Hyderabad.

Our mission is to ensure that drivers can travel with peace of mind, knowing help is just a call or tap away.

Our Services

Roadside Assistance

Immediate support for breakdowns and other emergencies.

Towing Services

Safe vehicle transportation to your preferred repair center.

Fuel Delivery

Running on empty? We’ll deliver fuel directly to you.

Battery Jumpstarts

Quick jumpstart services to get you back on the road.

Lockout Assistance

Locked out? Our technicians can help you regain access.
